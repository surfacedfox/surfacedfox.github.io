Hi, I'm Ashe (@surfacedfox)!


I'm a game designer and programmer! I also solve IRL problems using game tech at AxoMem. I love games, and I love everything about the art and craft of making them!

My skills and experience are the glue which hold projects together. I program systems, design workflows and art pipelines. While my deep expertise lies in game design and programming to support said design, I am also a broad-range generalist with skills in shader development, audio design, kitbashing, level design and art asset creation.

This site exists as an archive for projects which I can't necessarily release the source code for, but want to showcase nonetheless. It also hosts the authoritative, 'canon' versions of my various opinions which I think are worth sharing, or for some game design or development quick-tips and minor learnings which I might want to refer back to, down the line.
