Reclaiming myself

Sep 22, 2024 · surfacedfox

It is time to stop running.

Hey there. This thing still work?

It’s been quite a while since I was on here. My history tells me that the last time was all the way back in January, and even so, there’s hardly anything there that I’d consider noteworthy. So I guess we can start with a blank slate, now.

Lots has happened since then, and I can’t help but feel weirdly on edge, like things have fallen in place in a very specific way, as if the world is giving me a sign. Either way, what this means, dear reader, is I’m back for real. I’ve got things to do, and I’m pretty freaking excited to do ’em. There’s a part of me that I’d kept suppressed, hidden inside for so long that I’d forgotten that it even existed, and ya know what? I’m glad to have found it again.

Let’s shake the world.
